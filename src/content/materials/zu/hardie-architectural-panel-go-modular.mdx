# Hardie® Architectural Panel: Isixazululo Esisha Sokwakha Okumodular

## Ukubuka Kwephrojekthi

I-Beam Contracting ibambisene ne-Go Modular Technologies (UK) Ltd ukuze yakhe:

- I-flat eziyishumi ezimodular ePoole
- Izincazelo zokwakha ezisezingeni eliphezulu
- Umklamo wesimanje nowesimanje
- Izici ezikh inspired by indawo yasogwini
- Indlela yokwakha ehleliwe

## Izici Eziyinhloko

### Izincazelo Zephaneli
- Ukusebenza okwenziwe nge-A2
- Inqubo yokufaka elula
- Isixazululo esisebenzayo
- Izidingo zokmaintenance eziphansi
- Ukuphuma kokuthinta okuthambile

### Ukukhetha Umbala
- I-Arctic White
- I-Anthracite Grey
- I-Cobblestone
- Umbala ophumelelayo ohlotshiswe endaweni
- Ukubukeka kwesimanje

### Izinzuzo Zokufaka
- Inqubo yokufaka elula
- Uhlelo lokulethwa olwenziwe ngezinyathelo
- Amabhodi akhiwe ngaphambili
