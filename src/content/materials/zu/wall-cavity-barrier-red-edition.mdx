# I-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ihlangabezana nezinselelo zokwakha

Ezindlini eziphakathi nendawo kuya phezulu lapho ubuso bangaphandle buyi-masonry, ukwakhiwa kwezitini kungase kudinge ukwesekwa kwesakhiwo—ngokuvamile ngendlela yezinsika zensimbi. Nokho, lezi zinsika zokweseka zivame ukutholakala ngqo lapho i-cavity barrier kufanele ibekwe, kudala isimo esinzima sokufaka.

Ukuhlolwa kwakamuva kukhombisile ukuthi le nselelo inganqotshwa nge-AIM – Acoustic & Insulation Manufacturing’s **I-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition)**. Ethulwe ehlobo lika-2024, le mveliso entsha yakhelwe ukusetshenziswa njenge-cavity barrier noma i-cavity closer ngaphakathi kwesakhiwo sezingxenye zangaphandle, ivimbela ngempumelelo ukuhamba kokushisa, umlilo, kanye nomusi. Itholakala ezingeni lokushisa elingu-30, 60, noma 120 imizuzu, futhi izinga layo elandisiwe lokushisa lenza ilungele kokubili izicelo ezivundlile nezivundlile emigqeni yokuhlukaniswa komlilo ezindlini eziphakathi nendawo kuya phezulu.

Ukuze kubhekwane nobunzima bokufaka i-barrier ngokuhambisana nezinsika zokwakha, i-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ihlolwe nezinsika zokwakha ezakhiwe nguLeviat ngaphansi kwezimo ezinzima. Ukuhlolwa kwahluka ngezinga lokungena kwe-bracket ye-masonry kwi-barrier, futhi imiphumela iqinisekisile ukuthi i-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ingafinyelela imizuzu engu-120 ye-EI (Integrity and Insulation) ukusebenza.

"Umphumela wokuhlola ukuthi i-Wall Cavity Barrier yethu (Red Edition) ingafakwa phezulu noma phansi kwe-slab yophahla, nezinsika zokwakha ezihlolwe nge-50% kuya ku-140% yokungena emgqeni we-cavity barrier. Lokhu kunikeza abakhiqizi ukuhamba okukhulu kakhulu lapho behlanganisa kokubili i-shelf yokweseka ne-barrier," kuchaza umphathi wezohwebo we-AIM u-Ian Exall.

Ukuhlolwa kwenziwa ngokuhambisana ne-BS EN 1366-4:2021, imithetho evunyelwe yokumelana nomlilo ye-cavity barriers e-UK nase-EU. Ukuhlolwa okwengeziwe kufaka phakathi izinhlelo ze-masonry nezinsimbi (SFS), futhi i-AIM ifake imali ekuqinisekiseni kweqembu lesithathu okuvela ku-UKAS okukhokhelwa yi-IFC Certification Ltd kwezokwakha.

I-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) iyakwazi ukuvala izikhala ezifika ku-600mm ekwakhiweni kwe-masonry. Ihlolwe futhi ekwakhiweni kwe-SFS nase-rainscreen cladding. Le mveliso ilethwa ngendlela ye-slab, ivumela ukusika endaweni noma usayizi owenziwe ngaphambili. Itholakala ngama-slab angu-600mm no-1200mm ububanzi, ngezinketho zethikithi ezingu-75, 100, kanye no-125mm. Ngokuvamile, isetshenziswa ngokuhlanganyela ne-AIM’s Open State Cavity Barriers (OSCBs).

I-AIM ayinikezi kuphela le mveliso eqinile kodwa futhi inikeza ukwesekwa kwezobuchwepheshe okubanzi, okuhlanganisa nemininingwane ethile, ukuqeqeshwa, nokusiza endaweni. Ukuze uthole olunye ulwazi, kuhlanganise nemiyalelo yokufaka
