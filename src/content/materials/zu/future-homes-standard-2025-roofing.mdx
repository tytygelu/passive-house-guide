# Izindinganiso Zezindlu Zesikhathi Esizayo 2025: Ukuguqula Izindonga Nezokufaka

## Izidingo Eziyinhloko

Izindinganiso Zezindlu Zesikhathi Esizayo (FHS) zethula izidingo eziqinile zezindlu ezintsha ezakhiwe kusukela ngo-2025:

- Ukunciphisa okungama-75-80% ekukhishweni kwekhabhoni uma kuqhathaniswa nakuwo
- Izinhlelo zokuhambisa umoya ezithuthukisiwe
- Izindinganiso zokusebenza kwamandla ezithuthukisiwe
- Ukugxila endleleni yokwakha eqinile
- Ukusebenza kahle kokushisa okuhlelwe kahle
- Izinguquko ezithathwe ngababusi bomkhakha
- Indlela ebanzi yokusimama

## Izingxenye Eziyinhloko

### Izixazululo Zokuphahla
- Amathayili e-concrete njengenketho yendabuko
- Amathayili e-terracotta ukuze kube nesithakazelo
- Ama-slates e-fiber-cement athola ukwabelana kwemakethe
- Ukuhlela ukuhambisana kwamaphaneli elanga
- U-25% wokulahleka kokushisa ngaphezulu
- Ukuvulwa kahle kubalulekile
- Akukho zidingo zamaphaneli elanga eziphakanyisiwe okwamanje
- Ukuhlela okujwayelekile kokuhlanganiswa kwesikhathi esizayo

### Izinto Zobuso
- Izinketho zokwakha zentambo
- Ubuso bezindonga zamathe ukuze kuqinisekiswe ukuqina
- Izixazululo zokufaka i-vinyl
- Ukuhlanganiswa kwezinhlelo zensimbi
- Izinketho ze-weatherboard
- Izinzuzo ze-fiber cement:
  - Qinile futhi zisebenza ngezindlela eziningi
  - Ukuhlanganiswa okungaguquki
  - Ukusetshenziswa okuphansi kwezinto zokwakha
  - Ukunciphisa amandla ekukhiqizeni
  - Ukukhiqizwa kokuphuma okuncane
  - Ukuhlukaniswa komlilo kwe-A1
  - Ukumelana nezinga lokushisa eliphezulu
  - Izidingo zokmaintenance eziphansi

### Ukuhlanganiswa Okujwayelekile
- Ukuphuma komhlaba
- Izitezi eziphezulu ezine-cladding (isb., Cedral)
- Izindlela ezihlanganisiwe zezinto
- Ukuqinisekiswa kwesithakazelo
- Ukusebenza kahle

## Izindlela Zokufaka Izinsiza

### Ukufaka Izinsiza Zangaphandle
- Izinhlelo zokufaka i-rainscreen
- Ukusebenza kahle kwamandla okuthuthukisiwe
- Isikhathi sokuphila kobuso obwandisiwe
- Ukunciphisa ukucwebezela
- Ukunciphisa ukuhamba kwesakhiwo
- Izinzuzo zokuvikela kwesimo sezulu
- Ukunciphisa ibhuloho lokushisa

### Ukuvikelwa Kwangaphakathi
- Imineral wool rolls
- Izinhlelo zokwakha eziphakanyisiwe
- Isimo sezulu esizinzile ngaphakathi
- Ukubukeka kwangaphandle okuvikelwe
- Izixazululo ezisebenzayo
- Ukucabangela isikhala okudingekayo
- Izidingo zokufaka ezivunyelwe umlilo
