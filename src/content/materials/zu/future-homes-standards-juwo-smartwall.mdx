# Izindinganiso Zokwakha Izindlu Zesikhathi Esizayo 2025 kanye NeJuwo SmartWall Isixazululo

Izindinganiso ezintsha **Zokwakha Izindlu Zesikhathi Esizayo 2025** zihlose ukuguqula indlela esiyihlela ngayo futhi sakha ngayo izindlu ezintsha e-UK ngokuthuthukisa kakhulu ukusebenza kahle kokushisa nokunciphisa izindleko zokusebenza. Ngalesi sikhathi, lezi zindinganiso zifuna ukwehlisa umthelela wekhabhoni wezindlu ezintsha ngokubheka izinto ezibalulekile ezifana ne:

- **Ukukhishwa KweKhabhoni**
- **Ukusetshenziswa Kwamandla Okokuqala**
- **Ukusebenza Kahle KweFabric**

Uhlolo oluphakanyisiwe luzobheka ngokucophelela incazelo yeNotional Dwelling futhi luhlaziye amapharamitha abalulekile afana ne-U-values, ukuxhumana kokushisa (Psi Values), kanye nomthamo wokushisa wesakhiwo. Lezi zinto zithinta hhayi kuphela induduzo yangaphakathi kanye nokuthola ilanga, kodwa futhi nokuphephisa okuphelele kwempahla.

## Inselelo Yokwakha Kwenkolo

Ukwakha izindonga ze-masonry ezijwayelekile kuvame ukuba nezinselelo zokuhlangabezana nezidingo ze-U-values ezinzima ezidingekayo ngama-2025. Ukufeza izinga le-U-values (cishe 0.15 W/m²K) ngezindlela ezijwayelekile kungadinga ubukhulu bezindonga obukhulu—phezulu kwe-430–450 mm ngezikhala ezinkulu zokufaka—okuholela ezinkingeni zokwakha, ukukhuliswa kwezisekelo, kanye nezithuthukisi ezengeziwe.

## Izinzuzo zeJuwo SmartWall

Impendulo ingase ibe ezinhlelweni zokwakha ezisha ezifana ne-**Juwo SmartWall**. Le nqubo eyodwa ye-monolithic ihlanganisa ukufakwa kokushisa ngqo phakathi kwamabhulokhi e-clay, inciphisa ukuhamba kokushisa futhi ikhiphe isidingo se:

- Izikhala
- Izixhumi zezindonga
- Ukufakwa kokushisa okungeziwe

Ngokuhlanganisa ukufakwa kokushisa ngaphakathi kwebhulokhi uqobo futhi usebenzisa i-adhesive encane yokuhlanganisa, uhlelo lweJuwo SmartWall lunikeza isixazululo sokwakha esihlelelekile, esisebenzayo esihlangabezana nezindinganiso ezinzima zokusebenza kwamandla.

## Izinzuzo Eziyinhloko

- **Ukusebenza Kwezehlakalo Okukhulu:** Uthola ama-U-values aphansi njenge 0.11 W/m²K
- **Ukuhambisana Nezimo:** Kuhlangabezana futhi kudlula izidingo zomthetho wezokwakha
- **Ukukhula Okusheshayo:** Umklamo wedwala elilodwa uqhubekisela phambili isikhathi sokwakha
- **Indlela Yesimanje Yokwakha:** Isebenzisa ubuchwepheshe bokwakha obuncane be-mortar kanye nezinhlelo zokwakha eziphelele
- **Ukuqhubeka:** Isebenzisa i-clay—okwakhiwa kwemvelo, okuqhubekayo—ngokunciphisa ukusetshenziswa kwamanzi
- **Ukuguquguquka:** Ifaneleka kokubili ukuthuthukiswa okuphansi nokuphakeme, kanye nezinhlelo zokwakha ezizenzakalelayo
- **Ukuhlela Okulula:** Indlela yokwakha enganqamuki thermal bridge enezinselelo zokuhlela ezincane
