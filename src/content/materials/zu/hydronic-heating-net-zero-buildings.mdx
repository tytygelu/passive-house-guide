## Ukungena

Ukuphila ngokushisa kwamanzi kuyavela njengesixazululo esikhulu sokwakha izakhiwo ezinomthelela wokushisa okungalingani. Ekuseni lapho ukusetshenziswa kwamandla kungukuphakama, izinhlelo ezisekelwe ekushiseni ngamanzi zinikeza hhayi kuphela ukushisa indawo, kodwa futhi ukukhanya okuhlanganisiwe nekushisa kwamanzi okusetshenziswayo ekhaya. Njengoba uGary Perry weAltecnic ukuchaza, lezi zinhlelo ziyakhiwe ukunciphisa ukusetshenziswa kwamandla ngokusebenzisa izindlela zokubuyisela amandla nokuphatha amandla okujabulisayo ngenkathi kunciphisa ukuphumula ngaphakathi.

## Ukuphumula Kwezisebenzisi

Umlando ukubonisa ukuthi izindlela ezinikeza ukuphumula ukusetshenziswa kwamandla zingatholi kakhulu imakethe. Ukuphumula okuqinile kutholakala lapho ukushisa okukhiqizwa yizicubu kulinganiswa ngokukhipha kwakho. Izinhlelo zokusabalalisa ngamanzi ziyaphumelela kule ndawo ngokuthi:

- Ngokuhlanganisa ukushintsha izimo zomoya, izimo zezindawo, nokuhlukanisa kwamandla okushisa.
- Ngokunciphisa ukuphuma kwemoya nokugwema izimo zomoya ezifiselekile ezijwayelekile ngezinhlelo ezisekelwe emoyeni.
- Ngokusebenza ngokuthula, uqinisekisa ukuthi izinhlelo zokushisa nokukhanya azivexi indawo yokuphumula ekhaya.
## Ukuphathwa Kwe-Efficiency

Uma ukubumba izinhlelo zokushisa nokupholisa ezindlwini ezisebenzisa amandla aphansi noma ezinikeza amandla angu-zero, kubalulekile ukukhombisa amandla adingekayo ukusabalalisa amandla ashisayo. Khomba lokhu okulandelayo:

- Izinhlelo zokushisa ezijwayelekile zingase zisebenzise ama-circulators amancane (isibonelo, ama-circulators angu-4 nge-75 watts ngamunye) ukunikeza cishe i-100,000 Btu/hr, lokhu kwenza ukuphathwa kwe-efficiency kube cishe i-333.3 Btu/hr ngawunye we-watt.
- Ngokuphikisana, izinhlelo zokushisa ezintsha ze-'homerun' zisebenzisa i-circulator ephethe kahle, ephethwe yisivinini esishintshayo, esilawulwe yigunya. Lesi sici hhayi kuphela sikwenza ukuphathwa kwe-energy kubaluleke kodwa futhi sikugcina ukushisa kwe-tanki yokugcina (ngokuvamile kube ngu-120Â°F) okuhambisana kahle nezinhlelo ezinjenge-air-to-water noma water-to-water heat pumps.

Ababumbi kumele bazicabangele ukuthi ngamunye we-watt osebenzisiwe ukusabalalisa uya ekunikezeni ukulayisha kwamandla jikelele, okubaluleke kakhulu ezinhlelweni zokupholisa, lapho izilinganiso eziphezulu ze-airflow zingakwazi ukukhuphula ukusetshenziswa kwamandla ngendlela enkulu.
## Ukuphila Kwesistimu Nendlela Yokumelana Nayo

Okwamanje okukhulu okukhombisa ukuthi izinhlelo zezamanzi zingcono kakhulu ukuthi ziyadlula futhi zinamandla. Izinzuzo ezikhulu zifaka phakathi:

- **Ukuphila Okude:** Izicelo ezisetshenziswa kahle futhi ezinakekelwa kahle kusistimu ye-hydronic zingadlula izinyanga eziningi, ngokuvamile zidlula imithombo yokushisa noma yokukhanya ekhethiwe kuqala.
- **Ukukhokha Okude:** Kuphela eziningi zezinto zasendlini ezimanje ezinakekelwa ukuthi zingadlula isikhathi esifushane, izinhlelo zezamanzi zenzelwe ukuba zidlule, zicabangele ukuthi kungadingeki ukuthi zithathwe phansi njalo futhi zicabangele ukuthi zingaphazanyiswa izindawo zokuhlala.
- **Ukufaneleka Nokulungiswa:** Lezi zinhlelo zenzelwe ukuba zinamandla, zenza kube lula ukuzilungisa, noma yini ekuqhubekeni kweminyaka.

## Isiphetho

Izinhlelo zokushisa nezokukhanya zezamanzi zenza ukulinganisela okuhle phakathi kwendlela yokusetshenziswa kwamandla nokuthula kwezisebenzi zasekhaya. Ngokukhokha amandla kahle, ukusebenza ngokuthule, nokunikeza ukuphila okude, zenza isixazululo esihle kakhulu sezakhiwo ezinikezela amandla. Njengoba ukukhuthaza ukuncitshwa kwamandla nokudalula kwezinto ezahlukahlukene kuyanda, izinhlelo zezamanzi ziyaphuma phambili njengokukhetha okunamandla, okulungiselelwe ikusasa.
