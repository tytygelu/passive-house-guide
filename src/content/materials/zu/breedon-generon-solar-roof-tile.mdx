# I-Breedon Generon Solar Roof Tile: Isixazululo Esihlanganisiwe Sokwakhiwa Kwamandla

## Ukuhlanganiswa Kwelanga Okusha

- Amacell e-PV e-monocrystalline angu-3.2mm
- Hlanganiswe nezitezi ze-concrete ezingu-330mm x 420mm
- Ukufakwa okungaphazamiseki nezitezi ezijwayelekile
- Uhlelo lwezitezi ezingu-260 lwenza ukukhipha okujwayelekile kwe-4kW

- Uhlaka lweglasi oluqinile olufakwe ngokufanayo

- Ukubukeka okuqhubekayo kwephepha lephroof

✅ **Ukufakwa Okulula**  
Abakhi bezindlu bangafaka ngokusebenzisa:

✅ **Ukuphila Okuthuthukisiwe**  
Kuhlolwe ukumelana:

- Imiphumela ye-hail efana ne-baseball
