# Hardie® Architectural Panel: Isixazululo Esitsha Sokwakha Ngemodyuli

## Ulwazi lweProjekthi

IBeam Contracting ibambisene neGo Modular Technologies (UK) Ltd ukwakha:

- Iiflethi ezimodyuli eziyiNtlanu ePoole
- Iimfuno zokwakha eziphezulu
- Uyilo olutsha nolwakudala
- I-aesthetics ephefumlelwe yindawo eselunxwemeni
- Indlela yokwakha ePhased

## Iimpawu Eziphambili

### Iimfuno zePanel
- Ukusebenza kokhuseleko lomlilo kwe-A2
- Inkqubo yokufaka elula
- Isixazululo esisebenzayo
- Iimfuno zokugcina eziphantsi
- Ukuphuma komphunga okucacileyo

### Ukukhetha Umbala
- I-Arctic White
- I-Anthracite Grey
- I-Cobblestone
- Ipalethi ephefumlelweyo yendawo
- Ubukrelekrele besimanjemanje

### Ibenefithi Zokufaka
- Inkqubo elula yokufaka
- Inkqubo yokuhanjiswa kwezigaba
- Iipaneli ezikwi-pre-fabricated
- Ukuhamba kwesikhumbuzo sokwakha
- Ukukhetha kwemigangatho
