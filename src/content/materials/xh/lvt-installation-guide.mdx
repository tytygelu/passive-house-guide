# Iikhowudi zeVinyl eziphakamileyo (LVT): Umhlahlandlela opheleleyo wokufaka ukuze ufumane umphumo omhle

## Iimfuno eziBalulekileyo

Ukufakwa kwe-LVT kudinga ingqalelo ekhethekileyo kwiinkcukacha ngokwe-BS 8203:2017:

- Ukulungiselela i-subfloor ngokufanelekileyo
- Ulawulo olufanelekileyo lwe-moisuture
- Ukukhetha imveliso efanelekileyo
- Iindlela zokufaka ezichwepheshile
- Iindlela zokugqiba ezikumgangatho ophezulu

## Inqubo Yokufaka

### Ukulungiselela iSubfloor
- Ukususwa kwe-laitance ngoomatshini
- Ukususa iimpawu ezikwi surface
- Ukujonga ngokucokisekileyo umgangatho
- Ukujonga ubungakanani bokuphosa
- Iimigaqo yokulungiselela ezisiseko
- Ukuhambelana neMigaqo yeBritane

### Ulawulo lweMvula
- Ukuhlolwa kwemvula okuqhelekileyo
- Ukusebenzisa i-hygrometer e-calibrated
- Ukuhlola umgangatho we-RH (max 75%)
- Ukusetyenziswa kwememori engangeni manzi:
  - I-coat enye ye-RH ukuya kwi-98%
  - Ixesha lokuphilisa leeyure ezintathu
  - Ukuphumeza ukwahlula kwemvula ngokupheleleyo
  - Ukuthintela ukungasebenzi komgangatho

## Iimpawu Eziphambili

### Iziqhamo zePriming
- Ukulungiswa kwephezulu okubalulekileyo
- Ukusebenza kwecompound okujolise
- Ukuphathwa kwe-subfloor efunxa
- Iindlela zokonga ixesha zikhona
- Izicelo ezikhethekileyo:
  - Iiprimers zokujolisa ngokubanzi
  - Iiprimers zeephezulu ezingafunxi
  - Iiprimers ze-calcium sulphate screed

### Iimpawu zokuphosa
- Ukwakhiwa kobunzulu obuphezulu
- Ukuqinisekisa ukubonakala okuhle
- Iindlela ezinzima zikhona:
  - Amandla aphezulu okucindezela
  - Ukuzithambisa okuhle
  - Amandla okuthwala umthwalo
  - Ukumelana nokuhamba okuphezulu

### Iziqhamo eziLungileyo
- Izicelo ze-plywood subfloor
- Ukufakwa kwephezulu kwesinyithi
- Ukufaneleka kokuhamba
- Iindlela zokuthintela i-crack
- Ukugcina ukubonakala

## Iimfuno zeTekhnoloji

| Umsebenzi | Imfuno |
|-----------|--------|
| Umda we-RH | 75% standard, 98% nge-membrane |
| Ixesha lokuphilisa | Iiyure ezi-3 ze-membrane |
| Ukufikelela | Kuyahluka ngokwempahla |
| Iimfuno | I-BS 8203:2017 iyahambelana |
| Iintlobo zePhezulu | I-concrete, plywood, steel |
| Umgangatho wokuhamba | Iindlela ezinzima zikhona |

## Iindlela Zokufaka

### Ukusetyenziswa Kwe-Adhesive
- Iindlela ezithintela uxinzelelo
- Iimpawu zokufumana ngokukhawuleza
- Ukukwazi ukudala iipateni
- Iindlela ezifanelekileyo zokusebenzisa i-roller:
  - Ukusetyenziswa kwe-paint roller
  - Ukuphuculwa kwe-ridge
  - Ukuthintela iimpawu ze-trowel
  - Ukukhusela ubukrelekrele bokubukeka

### Ulawulo lweMgangatho
- Ukuqinisekiswa kokuhambelana
- Iziqhelo zomphakeli
- Ukubonisana ne-RAG
- Ngaphezulu kwe-5,000 kweefloorcoverings
- I-200+ yokufikelela komphakeli

## Iingxaki Ezivamile

### Iingxaki zeMvula
- Umphumo ophambili wokungaphumeleli kwefloor
- I-moisure yokwakha esele ikhona
- Iingxaki ze-damp eziphakamayo
- Ukuphuculwa kwe-adhesive
- Ukuphuka kwendawo
- Ukuphakamisa ukufakwa

### Iindlela Zokuthintela
- Ukuhlola umswakama ngokufanelekileyo
- Ukusetyenziswa kwe-membrane efanelekileyo
- Ukukhetha i-primer efanelekileyo
- Ukusetyenziswa kwe-compound efanelekileyo
- Ukufakwa ngochwepheshe

## Iindlela Ezingcono

### Iimfuno Zochwepheshe
- Ukuhambelana nemigangatho
- Ukukhetha amathuluzi afanelekileyo
- Ukusetyenziswa kwemveliso efanelekileyo
- Iindlela zolawulo lomgangatho
- Ukuhlola rhoqo

### Ukukhetha Iimathiriyeli
- Ukukhetha i-primer efanelekileyo
- Ukukhetha i-compound efanelekileyo
- Ukusetyenziswa kwe-adhesive ehambelanayo
- Ukuhlolwa komgangatho
- Iziqhelo zomphakeli

## Ibenefiti Zexesha Elide

### Iinzuzo Zokusebenza
- Ubomi bokufaka obude
- Ukugcina ubuhle
- Ukuqina kwesakhiwo
- Ukumelana nomjikelo
- Umthamo wokuthwala

### Ukusebenza KwezeMali
- Ukuncitshiswa kokugcinwa
- Ukuthintela ukuphindisela
- Ukuncitshiswa kokulungiswa
- Ubude bobomi
- Ukwaneliseka kwabathengi
