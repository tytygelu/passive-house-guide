# I-Breedon Generon Solar Roof Tile: Isixazululo Esihlanganisiweyo Sezibani Ezivuselelekayo

## Ukuhlanganiswa Kwamandla Elanga Okuyingqayizivele

I-Breedon Group ibambisene nomphakathi wezokwakha eYurophu, iTerran, ukuze iqhube iGeneron - uhlelo lwezibambo zephaneli ze-solar ezihlanganisiweyo ezihlanganisa:

- 3.2mm monocrystalline PV cells
- Ihlanganiswe ne-330mm x 420mm iithayile zecement
- Ukufakwa okungapheliyo kunye neethayile ezijolise kumgangatho
- Uhlelo lweethayile ezingu-260 lukhupha umphumo ojolise kwi-4kW

## Izinzuzo Eziphambili

✅ **Ukuhlanganiswa Kwezakhiwo**  
Kususa iipaneli ze-solar ezinzima ngokusebenzisa:
- Umphezulu weglasi otywiniweyo
- I-substrates zecement ezihambelanayo ngombala
- Umfanekiso ophakathi komphakamo wephepha

✅ **Ukufakwa Okulula**  
Abakhi bezindlu banokufaka usebenzisa:
- Iifasteners ze-storm clip ezijolise kumgangatho
- Uhlelo lokuhlanganisa olwakhiwe ngaphambili
- Akukho zixhobo zokufaka i-solar ezahlukeneyo

✅ **Ubulunga Obuphucukileyo**  
Uhlolwe ukuba lungamelana:
- Umoya ongu-120mph
- Iimpempe ezinkulu njengebhola ye-baseball
- Ubomi beethayile zecement obungama-50
- Iwaranti yokusebenza yeminyaka engama-20

## Ukuhambelana Nezikhokelo

Iphucula ukufezekisa iimfuno zeSigaba L ngokusebenzisa:
- Ukuveliswa kwamandla avuselelekayo kwi-site
- Ukuncitshiswa kokungcoliseka kwe-carbon
- Ukujolisa kumandla ngokuhlola ngokusebenzisa uhlelo lwezindlu

> "I-Generon imele utshintsho oluyintloko kwi-adoption ye-solar - ukwenza amandla avuselelekayo abe neempawu ezibonakalayo ngelixa kugcinwa ukusebenza komphakamo."
