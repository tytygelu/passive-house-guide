# I-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ihlangabezana nomngcipheko wokwakha

Ezindlini eziphakathi nendawo ukuya phezulu lapho ubuso bangaphandle bungumgobo, ukwakhiwa kwebhlogo kungadinga ukwesekwa kwesakhiwo—ngokuvamile ngendlela yezikhumbuzo zensimbi. Nokho, lezi zikhumbuzo zihlala zikhona lapho i-cavity barrier kufanele ibekwe khona, kudala isimo esinzima sokufaka.

Ukuhlolwa kwakamuva kukhombisile ukuthi le nkinga ingadluliswa nge-AIM – Acoustic & Insulation Manufacturing’s **I-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition)**. Ethulwe ehlobo lika-2024, lo mkhiqizo ophawulekayo wakhelwe ukusetshenziswa njengomgobo we-cavity noma umgobo ophakathi kwengxenye yangaphandle, uvimbela ngempumelelo ukuhamba kokushisa, umlilo, kanye nomsi. Utholakala ezingeni lomlilo leminithi engu-30, 60, noma 120, futhi izinga elandisiwe lomlilo lenza kube kuhle kokubili izicelo eziphakeme nezihamba phansi emigqeni yokuhlukaniswa komlilo ezindlini eziphakathi nendawo ukuya phezulu.

Ukuze kubhekwe ubunzima bokufaka umgobo ngokuhambisana nezikhumbuzo zokwakha, i-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ihlolwe nezikhumbuzo zokwakha ezakhiwe nguLeviat ngaphansi kwezimo ezinzima. Ukuhlolwa kwahluka ngezinga lokungena kwe-bracket yokwakha emgobeni, futhi imiphumela iqinisekisile ukuthi i-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ingafinyelela eminyakeni engu-120 ye-EI (Integrity and Insulation) yokusebenza.

"Umphumela wokuhlola ukuthi i-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ingafakwa phezulu noma phansi kwethala le-floor slab, nezikhumbuzo zokwakha zihlolwe ngokungena okungu-50% kuya ku-140% emgobeni we-cavity. Lokhu kunikeza abakhi ithuba elikhulu lokuhlanganisa zombili izikhumbuzo zokwakha nomgobo," kuchaza umphathi wezohwebo we-AIM u-Ian Exall.

Ukuhlolwa kwenziwa ngokuhambisana ne-BS EN 1366-4:2021, umthetho owamukelwe wokumelana nomlilo wezithiyo ze-cavity e-UK nase-EU. Ukuhlolwa okwengeziwe kufaka phakathi izinhlelo zokwakha ze-masonry nezinsimbi (SFS), futhi i-AIM ifake imali ekuqinisekiseni kweqembu lesithathu okuvela kwi-UKAS efanelekayo ye-IFC Certification Ltd yezenzo zokwakha.

I-Wall Cavity Barrier (Red Edition) ikwazi ukuhlinzeka ngama-voids afika ku-600mm ekwakheni kwe-masonry. Ihlolwe futhi ekwakhiweni kwe-SFS nase-rainscreen cladding. Lo mkhiqizo uhlinzekwa ngendlela ye-slab, uvumela ukusika endaweni noma usayizi owenziwe ngaphambili. Utholakala ngezikhumbuzo ezingu-600mm no-1200mm ububanzi, nezinketho zokuqina ezingu-75, 100, no-125mm. Ngokuvamile, isetshenziswa ngokuhlanganyela ne-AIM’s Open State Cavity Barriers (OSCBs).

I-AIM ayihlinzeki kuphela ngalo mkhiqizo oqinile kodwa futhi inikeza ukwesekwa kwezobuchwepheshe okuphelele, okuhlanganisa nezincazelo ezinemininingwane, ukuqeqeshwa, nokusiza endaweni. Ukuze uthole
