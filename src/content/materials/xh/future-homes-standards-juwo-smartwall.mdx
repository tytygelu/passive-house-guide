# Iimfuno zeZindlu zeFuture 2025 kunye neJuwo SmartWall Solution

Iimfuno ezintsha ze **Future Homes Standards 2025** zijolise ekuguquleni indlela esiyilayo kwaye sakha ngayo izindlu ezintsha e-UK ngokuphucula kakhulu ukusebenza kakuhle kokushisa kunye nokunciphisa iindleko zokusebenza. Ngale ndlela, iimfuno zifuna ukunciphisa umphumo wombane wezindlu ezintsha ngokujolisa kwiimeko ezibalulekileyo ezifana ne:

- **Ukukhutshwa kweCarbon**
- **Ukusetyenziswa kweMali yeSizathu**
- **Ukusebenza kakuhle kokushisa kweFabric**

Uhlolo oluphakanyisiweyo luza kujolisa kwiimfuno zeNotional Dwelling kwaye luhlolisise iiparameter ezibalulekileyo ezifana ne-U-values, ukuxhoma kokushisa (Psi Values), kunye nomthamo wokushisa wesakhiwo. Ezi zinto zineempembelelo ezingezizo kuphela kumphumo wangaphakathi kunye neenzuzo zeLanga, kodwa nakwisikhumbuzo esipheleleyo sokungena komoya kwindawo.

## Umngeni Wokwakha Ngendlela YesiNtu

Ukwakha iindonga ze-masonry ezisecaleni kwendlela yesiNtu ikakhulu ibhekisa kwiimfuno ezinzima ze-U-values ezifunwa ngamanqanaba ka-2025. Ukufezekisa i-U-values ezijolise kuyo (malunga ne-0.15 W/m²K) ngeendlela ezisetyenziswa ngokwesiko kungafuna ubukhulu obuphezulu bendonga—ngaphezulu kwe-430–450 mm kunye neekhonkco ezinkulu zokufudumeza—okubangela iingxaki kumyili, ukwandiswa kobungakanani beziseko, kunye nezikhuselo ezongezelelweyo.

## Ibenefithi yeJuwo SmartWall

Impendulo ingabakhona kwiinkqubo zokwakha ezintsha ezifana ne-**Juwo SmartWall**. Le nkqubo ye-monolithic single skin idibanisa ukufudumeza ngqo ngaphakathi kwiibhulokhi ze-clay, inciphisa ukuxhoma kokufudumeza kwaye ikhawuleze ukususa imfuneko ye:

- Iikhonkco
- Iindonga ezixhonyiweyo
- Ukufudumeza okongezelelweyo kwangaphandle

Ngokufaka ukufudumeza ngaphakathi kwi-blok uqobo kunye nokusebenzisa i-adhesive ye-thin-bed yokudibanisa, inkqubo yeJuwo SmartWall inika isisombululo sokwakha esilula, esisebenzayo, esihlangabezana nemigangatho eqinileyo yokusebenza kwamandla.

## Iinzuzo EziBalulekileyo

- **Ukusebenza Okugqwesileyo KwezeMali:** Ifikelela kwi-U-values eziphantsi njenge 0.11 W/m²K
- **Uhlangothi Lokuhlawula:** Ihlangabezana kwaye idlula iimfuno zomthetho wokwakha
- **Ukwakhiwa Okukhawulezayo:** Uyilo lwezindonga ezisodwa ezinzima lukhawulezisa ixesha lokwakha
- **Indlela Yesimanje Yokwakha:** Isebenzisa itekhnoloji ye-mortar ye-bhedi encinane kunye neepakethe zokwakha ezipheleleyo
- **Ukwenza Uhlanga:** Isebenzisa umhlaba—material emnandi, eyomeleleyo—ngokuncitshiswa kokusebenzisa amanzi
- **Ukuguquguquka:** Ifanelekile kwiiprojekthi zokuphakama eziphantsi neziphezulu, kunye neprojekthi zokwakha ezizimeleyo
- **Uhlalutyo Olulula:** Indlela yokwakha engenamathala okushisa enezithintelo zokuhlalutyo ezincinci
