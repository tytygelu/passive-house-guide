# IStandard yeZindlu zeMihla ezaKhalayo 2025: Ukuguqula iRoofing kunye neInsulation

## Imigaqo eBalulekileyo

IStandard yeZindlu zeMihla ezaKhalayo (FHS) iletha imigaqo eqinileyo kwiindawo ezintsha ezakhiwe ukusukela ngo-2025:

- Ukuncitshiswa kweemfuno ze-carbon emissions ngo-75-80% xa kuthelekiswa nezo zikhona
- Iinkqubo zokuphuma komoya eziphucukileyo
- Iimfuno zokusebenza kwamandla eziphucukileyo
- Ukugxininisa kwiindlela zefabric
- Ukusebenza kwe-thermal okuhlelwe kakuhle
- Utshintsho oluqhuba phambili ngababonisi bezeMveliso
- Umgaqo-nkqubo opheleleyo wokuhlala uhlaziyekile

## Iingxenye Eziphambili

### Izisombululo zePhepha
- Iitayile ze-concrete njengomphakathi wesiko
- Iitayile ze-teracotta zokuphucula ubuhle
- Iifayibha ze-cement ezithola umphakathi
- Ukuhlela ukuhambelana kweepaneli ze-solar
- I-25% yokulahleka kobushushu ngaphezulu
- Ukuventilation okufanelekileyo kubalulekile
- Akukho mfuneko yeepaneli ze-solar eziphoqayo okwamanje
- Ukuhlela okuqhelekileyo kokuhlanganiswa kwexesha elizayo

### Izinto zeFacade
- Iindlela zokwakha zeTimber
- Iifasadi zeLitye zokukhusela
- Iisombululo zeVinyl cladding
- Ukuhlanganiswa kweemeko zensimbi
- Iindlela zeWeatherboard
- Iinzuzo zeFayibha yeCement:
  - Iqinile kwaye iyakwazi
  - Umphezulu onokuphila
  - Ukusebenzisa izinto ezingaphezulu
  - Ukuncitshiswa kwamandla ekwenziweni
  - Ukuncitshiswa kokuphuma kwemfumba
  - I-A1 yokuhlola umlilo
  - Ukumelana nemozulu ephezulu
  - Iimfuno zokugcinwa eziphantsi

### Iintlanganiso eziqhelekileyo
- Ukuphosa komhlaba
- Iifoloko eziphezulu ezinecladding (umzekelo, Cedral)
- Iindlela ezixubeneyo zeematerial
- Ukujolisa kokubukeka
- Ukuphuculwa kokusebenza

## Iziqhamo zeInsulation

### I-Insulation yangaphandle
- Iisistim ze-rainscreen cladding
- Ukuphuculwa kokusebenza kwamandla
- Ukuphuculwa kobomi be-facade
- Ukuncitshiswa kokuncibilika
- Ukuncitshiswa kokuhamba kwesakhiwo
- Iinzuzo zokhuselo lwezulu
- Ukuncitshiswa kwe-thermal bridge

### Ukufakwa Kwangaphakathi
- Iimpahla ze-mineral wool
- Iinkqubo ze-wooden batten
- Umoya ongena ngaphakathi ozinzileyo
- Ukubonakala kokuphila okuqinisekisiweyo
- Iindlela ezixabisa imali
- Ukucaciswa kwesikhala kuyafuneka
- Iimfuno zokufaka ezivuthayo

|-----------|------------|

## Iimbono Zochwephesha

### Umbono we-Architect
- Indlela eqhutywa yinkqubo
- Ukugxila kwiimetri
- Ukugxininisa ukusebenza
- Ukubaluleka kokufumana i-accreditation
- Iimfuno zedokhumenti ezichanekileyo

### Iingcebiso ze-RIBA Survey
- Ukukhula kokuzibophezela kwi-sustainability
- Ukunyuka kokugxila kumyili ophantsi kwekhabhoni
- Ukuphuculwa kokwazi kwabamalungu
- Ukukhula komdla wabagibeli
- Ukucaciswa kweendleko zamandla

### Indima yoMphakathi
- Ukucaciswa komthombo wezinto
- Udocumetation yezenzo zokuqala
- Uhlolo lwe-carbon footprint
- Iziqinisekiso zokuhlala
- Iigaranti zokusebenza

## Imfuno yokufaka

### Iingcinga zabaFakeli
- Uhlaka lwezokuphefumla lwezobuchwephesha
- Ukukhetha izinto ezifanelekileyo
- Ukujolisa kwiinkqubo zokuhambelana
- Ukugxila kumsebenzi wesikhumbuzo
- Uhlaka lokugcina imihla ngemihla
- Uhlolo lokumelana nokushisa
- Ukuhambelana nemigangatho yokhuseleko lomlilo
- Uhlolo lwezimpilo
- Umsebenzi wokuphinda usebenzise

### Imfuno ezithile
- Ukucinga malunga neempahla ezishushu vs ezibandayo
- Uhlaka lwezokuphefumla olufanelekileyo
- Iindlela zokufaka ezisemgangathweni
- Ukuhambelana kwezinto
- Ukuhlanganiswa kwenkqubo
- Ukufikelela kokugcina kwixesha elizayo

## Umphumela weMvelo

### Iinzuzo ezikhawulezayo
- Ukuncitshiswa kokukhutshwa kwe-carbon
- Ukuncitshiswa kokusebenzisa amandla
- Ukusetyenziswa kwezinto ezihlala
- Ukuphuculwa kokusebenza kokushisa
- Ukuphuculwa kobudala bendlu

### Iinzuzo zexesha elide
- Ukuxhaswa komnotho ophindaphindayo
- Ukuncitshiswa komphumela wemvelo
- Ukuncitshiswa kweendleko zokusebenza
- Ukuphuculwa kwexabiso lempahla
- Ukwakhiwa okuqinisekisiweyo kwixesha elizayo

## Ikamva leMveliso

### Iimilo eziPhuhlayo
- Ukuphuculwa kwezemvelo ngokukhawuleza
- Utshintsho kumyili wekhaya
- Ukuphuculwa kokuphinda kusetyenziswe
- Ukugxininiswa kokusingqongileyo
- Ubugcisa kwiimathiriyeli
- Iindlela zokufaka eziphuculweyo

### Iziqinisekiso zoMvelisi
- Ukuphuculwa kokuphinda kusetyenziswe kwemveliso
- Ukuncitshiswa komphumo wokusingqongileyo
- Iimpawu eziphuculweyo zokuphucula
- Uphando kunye nophuhliso lwezixazululo
- Ubunkokheli bemboni
- Ukugxininiswa kuphando kunye nophuhliso
