Passive House—eyaziwa ngesiJamani njenge *Passivhaus*—imele indlela entsha yokwakha edumisa ukusebenza kakuhle kweenergi ngokugqithisileyo ngaphandle kokuphosa umphunga. Endaweni yokuthembela kuphela kwiindawo zokukhanya ze-solar, indlela ye-PH ihlanganisa iifiziksi zokwakha eziphucukileyo, i-superinsulation, kunye nokwakhiwa okungena moya ukuze kudalwe izakhiwo ezihlala zikhululekile unyaka wonke ngengxaki encinci yeenergi.

## Umlando Omfutshane kunye neMveliso

Uhambo lwaqala ngo-1990 ngempahla entsha eDarmstadt, eJamani—hhayi indlu enye, kodwa uchungechunge lwezindlu ezine. UDr. Wolfgang Feist kunye noProfesa Bo Adamson baphumelele kwiiprojekthi zokuqala ezi zaphula umbono wokuba iindawo zokwakha ezineenergi eziphantsi kakhulu zaziyingcinga kuphela. Nokuba emva kweminyaka engama-25, i-row houses zokuqala ziqhubeka nokusebenza ngokuhambelana neenkcukacha zazo zeenergi ezimangalisayo, zibonisa umthwalo omkhulu we-heat ophakathi kwe-10 W/m² kuphela. Le mveliso ibeke umgca ophucukileyo wokwakha iPassivhaus Institut (PHI) ngo-1996, ngoku ehola uphando kunye nemizamo yokufakela isiqinisekiso seendawo ze-PH kwihlabathi jikelele.
## Iimigaqo EziBalulekileyo zoMphakathi wePassive House

Kwi-ntshona yayo, umgangatho wePassive House ubhekiselele kwiimeko ezichanekileyo. Ngokusebenzisa imodeli ye-energi eneenkcukacha (ngokusebenzisa amathuluzi afana nePassive House Planning Package, okanye i-PHPP), abaphuhlisi bafikelela kwiindleko ze-energi ezimangalisayo—ukuphucula ukuya kwi-80% xa kuthelekiswa nezakhiwo ezijolise kumgangatho. Le ndlela igxininisa kwi:
- **Superinsulation:** Ukwakha i-envelopes ye-thermali ezomeleleyo ezinciphisa kakhulu ukulahleka kokushisa.
- **Airtightness:** Ukunciphisa ukuvuza komoya ukuze kugcinwe iimeko zaphakathi ezizinzileyo.
- **Optimized Ventilation:** Ukuqinisekisa umgangatho ophezulu womoya ophakathi ngelixa kuphinda kuthathwe amandla ngama-sistim okuphinda umphunga.
- **Renewable Integration:** Ukunika amandla okwenziwa kwendawo ye-net-zero energy production ngama-solutions e-renewable energy asebenzayo.

## Ukufikelela kweHlabathi kunye noLungiso

Oku kwaqala eJamani ngoku kusemhlabeni wonke. Ukusuka eYurophu ukuya eAsia, eMzantsi Melika, naseNyakatho Melika, umqondo wePassive House uhlelwe ukuze uhambelane neendawo ezahlukeneyo—ukusuka kwiindawo ezinomoya opholileyo eTokyo ukuya kwiindawo ezomileyo eLas Vegas. Iiprojekthi ezifana neBahnstadt yeHeidelberg zibonisa indlela iindawo ezipheleleyo ezinokuguqulwa ngaphantsi kwemigangatho ye-PH, zikhupha iindawo zokuhlala ezikhululekile, eziphantsi kwe-energi kumawaka abantu.

Kwiindawo ezifana ne-United States naseSweden, ukulungiswa kwendawo kuphumelele. Umzekelo, iPassive House Institute U.S. (PHIUS) kunye neSwedish Centre for Zero Energy ziye zaphucula umgangatho we-PH ukuze ziqwalasele iimeko zendawo, zibonisa ukuba ukuguquguquka kunye novavanyo kubalulekile ekuphumezeni ngempumelelo.
## Iinzuzo Ukugqitha Ekugcineni Amandla

Iinzuzo zomphakathi wePassive House zidlula kakhulu kwiimeko eziphantsi kwemali yokugcina amandla. Abahlali bajolise kumgangatho ophucukileyo wokuhlala, umoya ophucukileyo, kunye nendawo ekhuthaza impilo engcono kunye nokwandiswa kokusebenza. Ngaphezu koko, njengoko umphakathi wezokwakha ufuna iindlela zokunciphisa i-carbon, iiprojekthi zePassive House zisebenza njengezikhumbuzo zokuzinzileyo—zibonisa ukuba ukusebenza okuphezulu kunye nokuphathwa kwemvelo kungahamba ndawonye.

## Ukubheka Kwesikhathi Esizayo

Ukwandiswa komphakathi wePassive House kuqhubeka neentlobo ezinomtsalane ezifana nokwaziswa kweemveliso ze “Passive House Plus” kunye ne “Passive House Premium.” Ezi ntlobo zintsha azijolisi kuphela kwiimeko zokugcina amandla kodwa ziqwalasela nokukhiqiza amandla aphinda avuselelwe, ziqhuba izakhiwo ziye kwi-oversupply yamandla. Njengoko uluntu lwehlabathi lufuna ukuguqulwa kwemithombo yamandla, iPassive House ibekwe phambili—ibonelela ngendlela eqinisekisiweyo, enokuphinda isetshenziswe kwiindawo zokwakha ezine-net-zero energy.

Ekubambeni umphakathi wePassive House, abacwangcisi kunye nabaphuhlisi kwihlabathi jikelele abakhi nje izakhiwo; bakha iindawo ezomeleleyo, ezinzileyo ezijolise kwiimeko zezemvelo kunye nomnqweno wokuhlala kakuhle. Ikamva lokwakha likhona, kwaye likhumbuza ngempumelelo yokugcina amandla.
