Passive House—eyaziwa ngesiJalimane njenge *Passivhaus*—imele indlela entsha yokwakha eyamukela ukusebenza kahle kwamandla okukhulu ngaphandle kokwephula induduzo. Esikhundleni sokuthembela kuphela ekutholeni amandla elanga, indlela ye-PH ihlanganisa ubuchwepheshe bokwakha obuphambili, ukuvikela okukhulu, nokwakhiwa okungangeni moya ukuze kudalwe izakhiwo ezihlala zikhululekile unyaka wonke ngokufaka amandla okuncane.

## Umlando Omfushane Nokuthuthukiswa

Uhambo lwaqala ngo-1990 ngephrothothipi entsha eDarmstadt, eJalimane—hhayi indlu eyodwa, kodwa uchungechunge lwezindlu ezine. Ukuqalelekile nguDkt. Wolfgang Feist noProfesa Bo Adamson, lezi zinhlelo zokuqala zaphula umqondo wokuthi izakhiwo ezisebenzisa amandla aphansi kakhulu zaziyizimfundiso kuphela. Ngisho nangemva kweminyaka engama-25, izindlu zokuqala ziqhubeka nokusebenza ngokuhambisana nezibikezelo zazo zamandla ezimangalisayo, zikhombisa umthwalo wokushisa ophakeme ongu-10 W/m² kuphela. Le miphumela ibeke indlela yokwakhiwa kwePassivhaus Institut (PHI) ngo-1996, manje ehola ucwaningo nezinhlelo zokufakazela izakhiwo ze-PH emhlabeni jikelele.
## Izimiso Eziyinhloko Zokwakha I-Passive House

Ekuqaleni kwayo, i-Passive House standard igxile ekunembeni. Ngokusebenzisa imodeli ye-energi enembile (ngokusebenzisa amathuluzi afana ne-Passive House Planning Package, noma i-PHPP), abaklami bafinyelela izinzuzo ez remarkable ze-energi—kuze kube ngu-80% wokunciphisa uma kuqhathaniswa nezakhiwo ezijwayelekile. Le ndlela igxile ku:
- **Superinsulation:** Ukwakha ama-thermal envelopes aqinile anciphisa kakhulu ukulahleka kokushisa.
- **Airtightness:** Ukunciphisa ukuvuza komoya ukuze kugcinwe izimo zokuhlala ezihambisanayo.
- **Optimized Ventilation:** Ukuqinisekisa ikhwalithi engcono yomoya wangaphakathi ngenkathi sithola amandla ngokusebenzisa izinhlelo zokubuyisela ukushisa.
- **Renewable Integration:** Ukuvumela ukukhiqizwa kwamandla angama-net-zero endaweni ngokusebenzisa izixazululo zamandla avuselelekayo ezisebenza kahle.

## Ukufinyelela Kwamazwe Ngamazwe Nokuhlanganiswa

Okwaqala eJalimane manje sekus spread across continents. Kusukela eYurophu kuya e-Asia, eNyakatho Melika, naseNingizimu Melika, umqondo we-Passive House usuhlanganiswe ukuze uhambisane nezimo ezihlukahlukene—kusukela ezindaweni ezimanzi ze-subtropics zeTokyo kuya ezindaweni ezomile zeLas Vegas. Amaphrojekthi afana nesifunda saseHeidelberg’s Bahnstadt akhombisa ukuthi kanjani imiphakathi ephelele ingaguqulwa ngaphansi kwemigomo eqinile ye-PH, ihlinzeka ngezindawo zokuhlala ezikhululekile, ezisebenzisa amandla amancane kubantu abaningi.

Ezindaweni ezifana ne-United States naseSweden, ukuhlela kwendawo sekuphumelele. Isibonelo, i-Passive House Institute U.S. (PHIUS) kanye ne-Swedish Centre for Zero Energy baphucule i-PH standard ukuze baphendule izimo zomoya zendawo, beqinisekisa ukuthi ukuhamba phambili nokwakha okusha kubalulekile ekuphumeleleni kokufakwa.
## Izinzuzo Ngaphezu Kwezindleko Zamandla

Izinzuzo zokwakhiwa kwePassive House zidlulela kude kakhulu ngezindleko zamandla ezincishisiwe. Abahlali bajabulela induduzo ethuthukisiwe enomoya opholile, izinga eliphezulu lekhwalithi yomoya, kanye nezimo ezikhuthaza impilo engcono nokwandiswa kokukhiqiza. Ngaphezu kwalokho, njengoba umkhakha wezokwakha ufuna izindlela zokunciphisa ukungcoliswa, amaphrojekthi ePassive House asebenza njengemibhoshongo yokusimama—ekhombisa ukuthi ukusebenza okuphezulu nokuphathwa kwemvelo kungahamba ndawonye.

## Ukubheka Esikhathini Esizayo

Ukuthuthuka kokwakhiwa kwePassive House kuqhubeka nezinto ezijabulisayo ezifana nokwethulwa kwemibhalo ethi “Passive House Plus” kanye “Passive House Premium”. Lezi zihlukaniso ezintsha azicabangi kuphela ukusebenza kahle kwamandla kodwa futhi zicabangela ukukhiqizwa kwamandla avuselelekayo, ziqhuba izakhiwo ziye emandleni aphezulu. Njengoba umphakathi womhlaba uzama ukuguqula amandla abe yindlela esimeme, iPassive House ibheke phambili—ihlinzeka ngendlela eqinisekisiwe, engakhula ukuze ifinyelele ezakhiweni ezine-zero energy.

Ekuthatheni ifilosofi yePassive House, abaklami nabathuthukisi emhlabeni jikelele abakhulumi nje ngokwakha izakhiwo; bakha izindawo ezizinzile, ezisimeme ezibhekana nobunzima bezimo zokushisa kanye nesidingo sokuphila kahle. Ikusasa lokwakhiwa kuyaphuma, futhi kuhle kakhulu ngamandla.
